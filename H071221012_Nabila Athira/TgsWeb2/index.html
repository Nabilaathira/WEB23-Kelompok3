Iqbaal Dhiafakhri Ramadhan (born 28 December 1999) is an Indonesian actor and singer. Iqbaal began his career as an actor with his role as Trapani in the musical drama Laskar Pelangi in 2010. He then became a member of the vocal group Coboy Junior in 2011 until the group disbanded in 2017. He is known for his role as Dilan in the films Dilan 1990, Dilan 1991, and Milea: Voice of Dilan. In 2018, the man who is often called Iqbaal won an award at the International Film Festival & Awards Macao in the Variety Asian Star category.
